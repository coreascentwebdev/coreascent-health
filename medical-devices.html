Neuromodulation

Advanced Percutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation

A clinically proven, FDA-cleared neuromodulation therapy for chronic pain conditions. This minimally invasive, in-office treatment delivers targeted electrical stimulation to peripheral nerves, offering patients a non-pharmaceutical pathway to meaningful pain relief.

FDA-Cleared & Evidence-Based

Backed by Level 1 clinical evidence including double-blind, randomized, placebo-controlled trials demonstrating significant pain reduction.

In-Office Procedure

Performed as an outpatient procedure in a standard clinical setting — no hospital or surgical center required for most treatments.

Favorable Reimbursement

Covered by Medicare and commercial payers with established CPT codes. Strong reimbursement profile relative to device cost, with full RCM support available.

Non-Pharmaceutical Approach

Provides an alternative to long-term medication use for chronic pain, addressing growing demand for non-opioid treatment options.

Turnkey Support

Comprehensive billing, insurance verification, prior authorization, and claims management support so your practice can focus on patient care.

Equipment Financing

Flexible equipment financing options available through our capital partners, with terms aligned to your practice's reimbursement cycle.

Connected Medication Management

Smart Medication Adherence Technology

A connected, IoT-enabled medication management platform that helps patients stay on track with their prescriptions while giving providers real-time adherence data and remote therapeutic monitoring capabilities.

IoT-Connected Device

Cellular-enabled smart device syncs with a companion app to track medication schedules, send automated reminders, and log adherence data in real time.

Remote Therapeutic Monitoring

Integrates with RTM workflows, enabling providers to monitor patient adherence remotely and bill under applicable Medicare and commercial codes.

Provider Care Platform

Dashboard gives care teams visibility into patient medication adherence trends, enabling proactive interventions and improved outcomes tracking.

Patient-Friendly Design

Intuitive setup, audio-assisted guidance, and a companion mobile app make it accessible for patients of all ages and technical comfort levels.

Multi-Medication Tracking

Tracks multiple medications simultaneously with scheduled alerts, ensuring comprehensive adherence across complex medication regimens.

Dual Revenue Channel

Available for both provider deployment and direct-to-patient fulfillment, creating flexible distribution and revenue opportunities.
